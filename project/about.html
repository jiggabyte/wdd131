About Us

The Prof. Amaram Neighborhood Association is dedicated to creating a harmonious and safe environment for all residents of Akwakuma, Owerri North Local Government Area. Our key initiatives include:

  • Neighborhood Watch Program: Our active neighborhood watch program ensures the safety and security of all residents. Volunteers regularly patrol the area and coordinate with local law enforcement to maintain peace and order.
  • Electricity Problem Resolution: We promptly address any electricity issues in the neighborhood, working closely with the local electricity board to ensure consistent and reliable power supply.
  • Annual Neighborhood Parties: Our annual parties are a highlight, bringing together residents for a day of fun, food, and entertainment. These events strengthen our community bonds and celebrate our collective spirit.
  • Infrastructure Contributions and Fixes: Our association organizes fundraising drives and collaborative efforts to fix and improve local infrastructure, ensuring a well-maintained and beautiful neighborhood.
